Privacy Policy

Tetorica Retro Player

Tetorica Retro Player does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect user data.

This extension does not send browsing history, page content, audio, video, or personal information to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension may store user preferences locally using the Chrome Extension Storage API. These settings remain on the user's device and are not transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in future releases.
